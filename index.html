About Us

We propose systematic web-application for automating the management processes of the students ’ excuses reports. And it provides an appropriate solution to overcome the difficulties that students experience when submitting making excuses, so called (ARMS) , an acronym for Automated Academic Reports Management System. .

Aims

01

facilitate the access of students' excuses submitted to the lecturer in an electronic, easy, fast and uninterrupted way.

02

reduce the difficulties that students experience when handling reports.

03

enhance the monitoring of student’s absence reports, including following up and updating the submitted reports.

04

deliver the reports accurately.

05

reduce the demand resources in the college, i.e. human resources.
